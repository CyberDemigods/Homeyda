— About Us —

From Anahita's hands, with love

Anahita, born and raised in Shiraz, has been living with cream and fondant for four years now. From a small kitchen in Khaldebarin, she builds a handful of cakes a week to order — each one, by her own hands, made once, just for one person.

From vintage orders to minimalist bento cakes, from fondant toppers to wafer-paper flowers — every detail is hand-built, painstakingly, only for you.

We don't sell off-the-shelf cakes. Each order is a conversation — about the day that will be made sweet, the colours you love, the flavour that will linger as a memory.

— Anahita, Founder

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does payment take? +

SEPA Instant (Europe): 10 seconds. International transfer (SWIFT): 1–2 business days. PayPal: instant. As soon as we see the confirmation, we begin crafting your cake.

Can I pay by card? +

Yes. We work with our family partner in Italy, so you can pay with Visa, Mastercard, SEPA, or PayPal — exactly like ordering from any European business. Once the quote is confirmed, we send the payment details over direct message.

Is this method safe? +

Yes. Payment goes directly to an Italian bank account — the same way you'd pay any European business. We never see or store any of your card details.

Do you deliver to other cities? +

Yes — delivery to Tehran, Isfahan, Tabriz, Yazd, Mashhad, and other major cities is possible via Snappfood or Tipax. The delivery fee is included in the quote we send you.

What about privacy? +

Your identity stays private with us. We only need the recipient's name and the delivery address in Iran. Anything else — even your own name, if you prefer — is unnecessary.

How far in advance should I order? +

At least one week. For custom or intricate designs, two weeks. During peak seasons (Yalda, Nowruz, Mother's Day, Women's Day) please order three weeks ahead so we can secure your slot.
