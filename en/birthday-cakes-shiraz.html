Birthday Cakes in Shiraz

Homeyda is a home patisserie in Shiraz, in the Khaldebarin neighbourhood. We design and bake birthday cakes to order — from single-serve bento cakes for two, to themed cakes for children, to custom cakes for the big milestones. Every cake is made fresh, once, just for you.

Birthday cakes for every kind of celebration

Whatever the birthday, there is a cake for it. These are the styles our Shiraz customers order most:

  • Bento birthday cakes — a single-serve cake in a little box, perfect for an intimate birthday for one or two. The most budget-friendly way to order a birthday cake in Shiraz.
  • Kids' themed birthday cakes — characters, animals, dinosaurs, mermaids, race cars, football and more, hand-shaped in fondant or piped buttercream.
  • Milestone & adult birthday cakes — elegant, minimal, vintage or floral designs for 18th, 30th, 50th and every birthday in between.
  • Birthday cake & flower box — a cake paired with a fresh flower box, gift-wrapped for delivery.

Flavours & fillings

Choose from vanilla, chocolate, coffee, red velvet, and Persian favourites like pistachio & saffron and rosewater & cardamom — with fillings from fresh strawberries and cream to Raffaello and chocolate ganache.

How to order a birthday cake in Shiraz

Pick a design you love from our gallery, then message us on WhatsApp or Instagram with the date, size, theme and any message for the top. We confirm the details and price together, then bake it fresh. Please order at least one week ahead so we can give your cake the time it deserves.
